ABOUT US

At Amigo's Barber Shop, our goal is to provide a welcoming atmosphere to all our customers, extending friendly service and a helpful, accommodating approach. Aligning with South American customs, we strive to treat you as an 'Amigo' - a dear friend in Spanish.

Come and visit our establishment and show your support for your local barber. Please take a moment to unwind in our vintage traditional barber chairs, indulge in relaxation, and allow us to pamper you with our exceptional barbering services!

Embracing the Spanish saying, 'mi casa es su casa,' which translates to 'our home is your home,' we aim to create an inviting space where you feel at ease."

Our Staff Welcome You!

Our goal as staff is to make your barbershop experience the best it can possibly be! We guarantee you the best service quality from us. Please let us know if you have any concerns or doubts and casually speak to us.
